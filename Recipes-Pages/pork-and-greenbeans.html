Chinese Ground Pork and Green Beans Stir fry

Mindy's Ground Pork and Greenbeans

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce

Stir fry

  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 3/4 lb green beans
  • 6 cloves diced garlic
  • 1.5 tsp diced ginger
  • some chilies for spice

Finish

  • 1 tsp dark soy sauce
  • A pinch of salt to taste

Steps

  1. Prep
    • prepare sauce
    • wash and cut green beans
    • finely dice garlic and ginger
    • mince chilis - take out seeds for less heat
    • get dark soy sauce and salt ready
  2. Heat wok to medium heat, add oil and bring to temperature
  3. Add green beans, garlic, ginger and chilies until garlic and ginger smell delicious and fragrant
  4. Add the sauce to the wok and stir. Let the green beans soak up the sauce.
  5. When the green beans taste delicious, push them aside and add the pork.
  6. Breakup the pork, make sure there are no big chunks and mix with the green beans.
  7. Heat till you see no more pink.
  8. Finish the dish with dark soy sauce and a pinch of salt and cook for 30 seconds longer
  9. Serve over rice, noodles or even soups
